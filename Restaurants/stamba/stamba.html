Stamba

Best Tbilisi restaurant for: traditional dishes with a modern twist Situated in of one of Tbilisi’s best hotels, Cafe Stamba is a very Wes Anderson-esque affair, complete with a grandiose chandelier, iridescent tile work and a sage green and baby pink colour palette. While you can order pretty much any style of food here: pasta, ceviche and flavoursome salads, it’s the Georgian offering you’re going to want to indulge in. Boasting an avant-garde take on the country’s classic dishes – many prepared with herbs and vegetables from the hotel’s own micro-farm – Cafe Stamba’s unmissable dishes include three variations of the traditional khachapuri, a very moreish spiced green bean and walnut dip known as pkhali, and the buttery Georgian chicken soup chikhirtma. Address: 14 Merab Kostava St
